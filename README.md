# Study Notebook

A comprehensive HTML-based study notebook that helps you organize your learning journey with notes, flashcards, progress tracking, and scheduling.

## Features

### 📝 Notes
- **Add Notes**: Create study notes with titles, subjects, and content
- **Organize by Subject**: Categorize notes by Mathematics, Science, History, Literature, Computer Science, or Other
- **Search & Filter**: Find notes quickly using the search bar or filter by subject
- **Responsive Grid**: Notes are displayed in a responsive card grid layout
- **Local Storage**: All notes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage

### 🃏 Flashcards
- **Create Flashcards**: Add question/answer pairs for study review
- **Interactive Cards**: Click to flip between question and answer
- **Navigation**: Browse through your flashcard collection with Previous/Next buttons
- **3D Flip Animation**: Smooth card flipping animation for better visual experience

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- **Study Statistics**: Track total notes, flashcards, subjects, and study streak
- **Subject Breakdown**: Visual progress bars showing distribution of notes by subject
- **Real-time Updates**: Statistics update automatically as you add content

### 📅 Study Schedule
- **Plan Study Sessions**: Schedule study tasks with date, time, and subject
- **Task Management**: Mark tasks as complete or delete them
- **Organized Display**: Schedule items are sorted chronologically
- **Progress Tracking**: Keep track of completed vs pending tasks

## How to Use

1. **Open the Notebook**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Add Your First Note**: 
   - Go to the Notes tab
   - Fill in the title, select a subject, and write your content
   - Click "Add Note"
3. **Create Flashcards**:
   - Switch to the Flashcards tab
   - Enter a question in the "Front" field
   - Enter the answer in the "Back" field
   - Click "Add Flashcard"
4. **Schedule Study Time**:
   - Go to the Schedule tab
   - Set a date, time, subject, and task description
   - Click "Add to Schedule"
5. **Track Progress**:
   - Visit the Progress tab to see your study statistics
   - View how your notes are distributed across subjects

## Technical Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Local Storage**: All data is saved locally in your browser
- **Modern UI**: Clean, gradient-based design with smooth animations
- **No Dependencies**: Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no external libraries needed
- **Cross-browser Compatible**: Works in all modern web browsers

## Data Persistence

Your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage, which means:
- ✅ Data persists between browser sessions
- ✅ No internet connection required
- ⚠️ Data is tied to the specific browser and device
- ⚠️ Clearing browser data will remove your notes
